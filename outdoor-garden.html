Outdoor zone • Garden highlights
Explore objects placed among plants and pathways. Tap an artefact to see how it connects to daily life, ritual or performance.
Wooden Shrine Figure
Wood, pigment • Southern Nigeria • 19th–20th century

This carved figure likely stood in a family or village shrine. Its upright pose and layered neck rings emphasise dignity, beauty and spiritual strength.

Mask & Calabash Rattle
Wood, gourd, pigment • West Africa • 20th century

Placed together, the mask and rattle hint at sound, movement and storytelling. They echo the energy of outdoor festivals and night-time performances.

Terracotta Guardian Figure
Terracotta • Central Nigeria • Nok-inspired style

With its bold features and patterned body, this figure recalls ancient Nok terracotta sculptures that once guarded compounds and sacred spaces.
